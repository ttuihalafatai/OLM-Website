Aloha!

Welcome to Our Lady of the Mount Catholic Church! We are located in the beautiful Kalihi Valley on Oahu. We welcome all new parishioners and we invite you to join us in worship!

Our Mission

We, the Our Lady of the Mount parishioners are a multicultural welcoming parish; are inspired by the Holy Spirit to worship God and committed to serve the community through faith, youth formation, stewardship, fellowship, and outreach.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant faith community with active parishioners, working together in unity to know and love God and to serve Him through one another.

Our Core Values

  • Community Building
  • Commitment
  • Service
  • Stewardship
  • Unity
  • Welcoming
  • Youth
  • Spirituality
