Sacraments

Baptism

If you would like to have your child baptized, please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC, at 808-845-0828, Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. Obtain an application form at the parish office during office hours, Monday to Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. and Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. The office is closed on Saturday, Sunday, all Holy Days, State and Federal holidays. An interview is scheduled on the second Saturday of the month. At that time, you must bring all required documentation. A Pre-Baptismal Catechesis for the parents and godparents is on the third Tuesday of the Month at 5:00 p.m. in the Parish Hall. Baptisms are held every 4th Sunday of the month at 10:00 a.m. after the 8:30 a.m. Mass. It is mandatory that the parents and godparents attend the 8:30 a.m. Mass before the Baptism. Baptisms are not performed during Lent.

For information regarding adult baptisms, please contact Ms. Fualupe Tuihalafatai, RCIA Coordinator, at 808-845-0828.

Confession/Reconciliation

Saturday 5:00 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. or by appointment. Please call the office.

First Holy Communion

If you would like to receive First Communion, please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC, at 808-845-0828 during office hours. Candidates must be baptized and have received at least two years of catechism.

Confirmation

To learn more about Confirmation training, please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC at 808-845-0828 during office hours.

Marriage

Couples are required to make arrangements at least four months before the wedding date. Diocesan policy requires couples to attend an Engagement Encounter Weekend. Please contact the office at 808-845-0828 for more information.

Anointing of the Sick

Please call the office for a priest. For emergency at the hospitals, please call 808-597-8779. Fr. Francis Sanchez and Fr. Teddy Gaquit are the Catholic Chaplains for Queen’s, Straub, Kapiolani and Kaiser hospitals.

Wake and Funeral Services

Please contact the office at 808-845-0828 before making arrangements with the mortuary.
